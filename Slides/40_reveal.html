  <p>The Lorenz Equations</p>
  
  \[\begin{aligned}
  
  \dot{x} & = \sigma(y-x) \\
  \dot{y} & = \rho x - y - xz \\
  \dot{z} & = -\beta z + xy
  
  \end{aligned} \]
